The Genius Fund: A New System for Human Potential.

We are building a global talent discovery engine to identify the top 0.1% of foundational genius—wherever it exists—and provide a fully-funded launchpad: education, mentorship, and capital.

1. SCOUT

Our decentralized network of Apollo Scouts uses gamified challenges to identify hidden genius in remote communities.

2. LAUNCH

3. EMPOWER

We provide seed funding and lifelong support to help our fellows launch startups, lead communities, and solve the world's toughest challenges.
